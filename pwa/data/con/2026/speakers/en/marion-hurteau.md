## About Marion

Marion discovered programming in high school, where she coded a small robot in C. She hasn't stopped developing since, with a particular interest in everything related to language. She has been working with PHP **for over 10 years**, specializing in the Symfony framework, and now holds the position of Senior Developer at Les-Tilleuls.coop.

She attended her first conferences at FOSDEM in 2017, and that was the turning point: she realized **the importance of open source** and wanted to contribute to the community. Today, she is delighted to share her own experience at conferences.

When she isn't coding, you can find her drawing, working out, or hunting dragons.
